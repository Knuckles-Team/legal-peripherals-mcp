# Legal Peripherals MCP

Welcome to the documentation for the Legal Peripherals MCP Server. This server facilitates automation of essential legal and corporate workflow actions, including:
- **Secretary of State (SOS) Crawlers**: Automated business registration checks across all 50 states.
- **EIN & Form SS-4 filing**: Automatic PDF drafting and filing scheduling to respect IRS business hours.
- **Statute Catalog & Templates**: Programmatic lookup of corporate/LLC statutes and document drafting templates.
